Liaoning tame Speedy Tigresses again

KUALA LUMPUR: The national women's hockey team have suffered their third loss to Liaoning province in the tour of China.

This time, it was a 2-0 defeat to Liaoning on Tuesday in their fifth friendly at Gongshu Hockey Stadium.

Liaoning scored both their goals in the third quarter.

World No 23 Malaysia are preparing for the Nations Cup in Poland next month.
